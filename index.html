Come practise your English with us

Our strategy

What you need to actually become a fluent speaker in English is not study grammer, you need only need a lot of speaking and listening practices.

Listening practices

You'll have three kinds of listening: My story, Inspiration Persons and The World We Live. . Each listen's a particular purporse designed to complete the set of English's fluency requierements.

Speaking practices

For each listening, you'll have to sort of debreefing: mate-to-mate and Telegram presenter. In the mate-to-mate sessions, you'll share with a collegue what you've learned through your last listening.. In the persenter sessions, you'll present a recent topic by a Telegram live video.

The goal of the speaking practices is to persuade your listener(s) to find out more about your topic.

My story

The path you tread, we treaded ourselves.


We want you to feel confident in your learning process with us. "My story" listens are rich with a lot of hand-on advices to help you become autonomous and confident at learning a language.

Inspirational Personages

We select great motivational biographies like "Abranham Lincoln". This inspirational stories will shape your personality. This way, you'll be not only learning English, but more importantly, you'll be learning how to get the right midsets for you personal life goals.

The World We live

We want you to feel comfortable with a variety of topic, that's why "The World We Live" section exists. It's about real facts worldwide, a sort of documentary.
