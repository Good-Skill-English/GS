What is Good-Skill Show ?

Good-Skill Show's a public Telegram group as part of a full course we're providing for everyone dreaming about reaching English fluency.

One of the things that make people straggle with learning English is the lack of English envorionnement. That's where Good-Skill Show comes in. It's an English envorionnement for brashing on your listening, writing and reading skills.

There's a weekly Telegram live, that we call "a show", presented by our members on a variaty of topics. By following these shows, you'll highly enchance your listening, as well as speaking skills through your questions at the end of the live.

Why this course ?

As many people around the World, I've spent more than 7 year relying on the school to speak English fluently. However, the thing's that it just doesn't work as it would. The educational system lacks the very basics one needs to reach fluency:

“more speaking, more listening, less grammer ”.


At Good-skill English, we're committed to providing you with a full immersion English expirience on speaking and listening, the same baby-experiency used to learn your native language.
